EyeContact

3D Artist (i.e. 90% sourcing for free models 10% trying to model stuff I can't find in Maya)

Eye Contact is a first person multiplayer VR game. Players takes on the role of humans have to steal a precious item and escape from a wax museum while being hunted by monsters. Humans have to make use of the environment to stop the monsters from getting close. Monsters on the other hand can move fast but have one key vulnerability: they cannot move while being seen by any other player.

Last Ward

Artist, "Programmer"haha RPGmaker

Last Ward is a horror JRPG with a local twist. You play as a girl who wakes up to find herself trapped in a decrepit hospital. As she explores the place and solves various puzzles to escape, she uncovers more of the place's dark story - and its gruesome inhabitant. Will you be able to guide her to safety? Or will she fall prey to one of the other four possible grim endings?
